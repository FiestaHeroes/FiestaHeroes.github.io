Fiesta Heroes, Open source project for developing and maintaining "Fiesta Online" server file sets. While also documenting information and preserving assets.


Development • Documentation • Preservation

Development

You may be wondering what this project is all about. It began when a group of individuals from the private server development community came together to form this project. Our goal is to provide the best foundation for creating your own server. We are dedicated to developing, maintaining, and fixing any retail or base content within our sets. Our platform offers a variety of sets to choose from, such as NA2016, CN2012, or even TW2008, allowing you to start developing your server immediately. Our team is dedicated to making this process as seamless as possible. Members of the community will no longer need to search various forums or Discord channels for fixes and updates. Instead, the community can report issues through our GitHub or Discord, where we will do our best to address issues and create a stable base for everyone.

Documentation

In addition, we have created our own documentation site, with the goal of providing a public and well-organized resource for guided development.

Preservation

Furthermore, we are also hosting our own archive, where we store official content from over the years, such as images, articles, videos, clients, tools, and more.
